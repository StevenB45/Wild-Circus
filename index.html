The show

Wild Circus presents its futuristic show "The Wild - Reinvent the Circus" Free your mind, let yourself be carried to the future and watch the most incredibles artists evolve with the latest technological innovations.

A huge infrastructure, more than 100 technicians accompany 42 artists to create the dreams of more than 4000 people every night. After traveling all over the world, in 2016 the Wild Circus becomes resident for 5 years on the stage of the Caesar Palas in Las Vegas for the most visionary and ambitious show ever imagined.

Between robots, holograms, 3D projections and walls of water, come to discover the circus of tomorrow ... Today.
